About Us

We are a hospital queue reducing company that uses priority scheduling special algorithms to provide efficient service to our customers.

Our algorithms take into account the patient's urgency, their medical condition, and their wait time to determine the optimal order for their treatment.

This allows us to reduce wait times for patients, improve patient satisfaction, and save hospitals money.

We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible service. We are always looking for ways to improve our algorithms and our customer experience.

If you have any questions or feedback, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Our Services

We offer a variety of services to help hospitals reduce wait times and improve patient satisfaction.

  • Queue management software: Our software can track patient queues, manage appointments, and provide real-time queue status information to patients.
  • Virtual queuing: Patients can join the queue from anywhere, using their smartphone or computer. This can help to reduce the number of people waiting in the hospital lobby.
  • SMS notifications: Patients can receive SMS notifications when their turn is approaching. This can help them to stay informed and avoid long wait times.
  • Loyalty programs: Patients can earn points for using our queue management services. These points can be redeemed for discounts on future medical care.
  • Reporting and analytics: We provide hospitals with reports and analytics on their queue management data. This data can be used to improve patient flow, staff efficiency, and hospital revenue.

We also offer consulting, training, and support services to help hospitals implement our queue management solutions.

If you are a hospital looking to improve your queue management, please contact us today. We would be happy to discuss how our services can help you.
